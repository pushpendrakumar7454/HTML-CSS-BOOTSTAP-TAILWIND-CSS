BENEFITS

Smart City offers a competitive compensation package commensurate with experience and performance. In addition to base salary, many positions offer additional incentives for outstanding team and individual contributions. We provide our full-time team members with the resources they need, both in and out of the workplace. Some of our benefits include health and dental insurance, medical spending and dependent care spending accounts, basic life and supplemental life insurance, long-term disability coverage, and 401(k) savings plan. Smart City is an Equal Opportunity Employer and a Drug & Smoke-Free Workplace.
